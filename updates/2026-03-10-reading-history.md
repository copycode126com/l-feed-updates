We just shipped **Reading History**.

Now you can track every article you've read and easily continue your learning journey.

## Improvements

- Faster article loading
- Better mobile layout
- Continue reading from where you stopped

## Why we built this

Learners asked for a single place to review the posts they already studied. Reading History keeps a lightweight log that syncs across devices.

## What's next

- Filter history by level
- Export learning streaks for social sharing
- Email reminders when new stories match your pace
